ABOUT US

We love shooting

Once upon a time, three random ballistic nerds connected on X (formerly Twitter) and started trading thoughts and ideas in regard to becoming better shooters and firearms instructors. As time passed, the three became friends and eventually met in real life. On a seemingly normal evening, the three decided to create a space on X to pass the time and answer questions about shooting. The space ended up being a lot more fun than they expected. And so, a weekly recurring space on X dubbed ShotCraft was born...
