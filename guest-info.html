ABOUT THE SPACE

Almost Every Wednesday @ 8PM Central on X (formerly Twitter)

Almost because there are holidays, life happens, and what not, but we aim to host the ShotCraft space on X (formerly Twitter) every Wednesday at 8 PM. Wel also do our best to keep the conversation to no more than an hour.

Prior to your scheduled space, one of the hosts will be in contact with you to plan your space. This will include some topic brainstorming which will give all of us a chance to prepare so don't appear to be a bunch of blundering nitwits during the live discussion.

WHAT YOU WILL NEED

PREREQUISITES

Technology can be a pain in buttocks


As we host more spaces we figure out things that work and things that don't. As we learn those things, we put them here to help make it as easy as possible to for you to have everything you need for a pleasant and successful guest appearance.

  • Install the X app on your mobile device or tablet
    X for iOS or X for Android
    It's okay to keep a desktop browser nearby to catch posts on X regarding the space, but we have found that listening or speaking from a desktop browser is finicky at best.
  • Find a comfortable relatively quite space
    It's an hour-long conversation. Pick a spot where you can listen and talk comfortably.
  • Consider using headphones
    Depending on your environment, you may find using a set of headphones will make listening more enjoyable.
  • Consider using a microphone
    The microphones on mobile devices work okay, but they aren't always the best. They often pick up background noise as well. Headphones with built in microphones can sometimes work better. A dedicated microphone will almost always work best, but it isn't required.

What a typical space is like

This should give you an idea of what to expect.

  • 7:55 PM
    You will receive a direct message (DM) with a speaker invitation to the space. Click/tap it to join the space.
  • 8:00 PM
    The host will welcome the audience and invite you to introduce yourself.
  • 8:10 PM
    We will jump into the main topic(s). The host will query the co-hosts for follow-up commentary or questions.
  • 8:40 PM
    We will wrap up the main topic(s) and invite the audience as speakers for interactive Q&A.
  • 8:55 PM
    You will be invited to plug anything you have coming up or have going on.
  • 9:00 PM
    Final space announcements and space is concluded.
